About the Destination

Where Alpine Tradition Meets World-Famous Scenery

Zermatt is one of Switzerland's most iconic mountain destinations, located at the foot of the Matterhorn. The village is famous for its car-free atmosphere, traditional alpine architecture and breathtaking natural landscapes.

Visitors enjoy a unique combination of outdoor adventure, Swiss culture and premium hospitality throughout the year. Whether in winter or summer, Zermatt offers unforgettable experiences in the heart of the Alps.

Tourism Management

How Zermatt is Promoted Worldwide

Zermatt Tourism is the official destination management organisation responsible for promoting and coordinating tourism in the region.

Their mission is to maintain Zermatt's position as one of the world's leading alpine destinations while protecting its unique character and natural environment.
