Getting There

Transport to Zermatt

Zermatt is a car-free village – all visitors must park in Täsch and take the Matterhorn Gotthard Bahn train for the final 12-minute journey. This is part of the experience!


  • Swiss Federal Railways (SBB): Main connections from all Swiss cities
  • Matterhorn Gotthard Bahn: Final connection Täsch → Zermatt (12 min)
  • Glacier Express: Scenic journey from St. Moritz (7.5 hours)
  • Swiss Travel Pass: Unlimited travel – highly recommended
  • Parking available in Täsch: from CHF 15 per day

Insider Tip

For the best Matterhorn views, book a hotel on the east side of the village. The mountain faces east, meaning you will wake up to the Matterhorn catching the first morning light – one of the most spectacular sights in the Alps.


Always book accommodation at least 3 months in advance for the Christmas/New Year period and February half-term – these are the busiest weeks of the year.
