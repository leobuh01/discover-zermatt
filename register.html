Our Visitor Engagement Strategy

As Secretary of Tourism, we use the registration data to guide potential visitors through a carefully designed conversion journey.

01

Welcome Email

Immediate personalised welcome with curated top 5 suggestions based on their interests + early-bird discount code.

02

Segmented Campaigns

Monthly newsletters tailored by profile: ski enthusiasts, luxury travellers, families – each receiving relevant content.

03

Retargeting Ads

Non-bookers are retargeted via Instagram, Facebook and Google Display Ads with personalised offers.

04

Loyalty Programme

Post-visit follow-up email with review request + loyalty discount encourages repeat tourism.
