Winter · Year-Round

Ski & Snowboard

The Matterhorn Ski Paradise is one of the largest ski areas in the Alps, with over 360km of slopes connecting Zermatt (Switzerland) and Cervinia (Italy). With guaranteed snow and glacier skiing available year-round, Zermatt is a skier's paradise for all levels.

  • 360 km of slopes across two countries
  • Highest ski area in the Alps – up to 3,883 m
  • Year-round glacier skiing available
  • Ski schools and rental shops throughout the village
  • Children under 9 ski free with a paying adult

Summer · Spring · Autumn

Hiking & Mountain Trails

With over 400km of marked hiking trails, Zermatt is one of the best hiking destinations in Switzerland. From easy valley walks to challenging high-altitude routes, there is a trail for every fitness level.

  • 400+ km of marked trails
  • 5 Lakes Walk – one of Switzerland's most scenic hikes
  • High-altitude routes up to 3,400 m
  • Guided tours available for all levels
  • Trail maps available at the tourist office
For Thrill-Seekers

Adventure Activities

Beyond skiing and hiking, Zermatt offers a wide range of activities for outdoor enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

Summer

Mountain Biking

150+ km of dedicated mountain biking trails with lift access to higher routes.

Summer

Climbing

Rock climbing and mountaineering for all levels, with certified guides available.

Summer

Paragliding

Tandem paragliding with panoramic Matterhorn views – a truly unforgettable experience.

All Year

Glacier Trekking

Explore the glaciers of the Matterhorn Glacier Paradise with certified mountain guides.

Rest & Recover

Wellness & Relaxation

After a day in the mountains, Zermatt's exceptional wellness facilities provide the perfect way to unwind. From luxury hotel spas to fine alpine dining, the village caters to every comfort.

  • Luxury hotel spas with mountain views
  • Thermal baths and saunas
  • Yoga retreats and mindfulness experiences
  • Fine dining and traditional Swiss cuisine
  • Après-ski bars and cosy mountain restaurants
